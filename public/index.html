Mastermind


There is a secret code, made up of a random combination of 4 balls with 6 possible colors.
You must try to find the correct combination with a few guesses as you can.
Each time you guess you will be told how many of the balls are:
  • Of the right color and in the right position.
    • or...
  • Of the right color but in the wrong position.
Think carefully! If you haven't cracked the code after your 15th guess, you lose!
