Mastermind

There is a secret code, made up of a random combination of 4 balls with 6 possible colors. You must try to find the correct combination with a few guesses as you can. Each time you guess you will be told how many of the balls are:
  • Of the correct color and in the correct position.
    • or...
  • Of the correct color but in the wrong position.
Think carefully! If you haven't cracked the code after your 15th guess, you lose!
